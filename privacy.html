Privacy Policy

At NexGen Rewards, we take your privacy and data security seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our survey portal.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data required to maintain a secure and functional research environment. This includes your unique Worker ID, IP address, device type, and session metadata. We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII) such as your full name, physical address, or phone number directly on our servers.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Maintaining secure user sessions and preventing fraud.
  • Verifying survey completions with our research partner, TheoremReach.
  • Analyzing portal performance and improving user experience.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Your anonymized survey responses and demographic data are shared with our partner, TheoremReach, for market research purposes. TheoremReach operates under strict global privacy standards. We do not sell your tracking data to any other third-party marketing firms.

4. Data Security & Encryption

All data transmitted through our portal is protected by industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption. We further secure session tracking using SHA1-based hashing to ensure that your Worker ID cannot be intercepted or manipulated during transmission.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential security cookies to track your session status and ensure you are not using multiple accounts on a single device. These cookies are mandatory for the portal's security framework.

6. Data Retention

Session logs are periodically purged to ensure user privacy. You may request the deletion of your specific Worker ID log by contacting our support team at username47397@gmail.com.

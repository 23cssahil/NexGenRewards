Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

NexGen Rewards ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our survey portal.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to facilitate survey access and reward processing:

  • Identifier Information: Worker IDs provided by users for tracking rewards.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, device types, and browser information (processed via Cloudflare for security and fraud prevention).
  • Activity Data: Timestamp of survey starts and completions.

2. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share user identifiers and demographic data with our primary research partner, TheoremReach (A RIWI Company), to enable survey opportunities. We do not sell your personal contact information to third-party marketers.

3. Data Protection (GDPR/CCPA Compliance)

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All research data is handled anonymously. Users have the right to request the deletion of their portal activity records by contacting us.

4. Use of Cookies & Security

We use essential cookies and security challenges (Cloudflare Turnstile) to ensure a secure environment and prevent automated bot access.
